Tucán & Nearby Tourist Activities

Palash Tower enjoys the unique advantage of its proximity to Tucán Country Club & Resort, a lush enclave known for its golf course, tranquil landscapes, and resort-style atmosphere. Residents can enjoy green open spaces, fresh air, and a relaxed lifestyle just minutes from the city.

Activities for Residents & Visitors

  • Hiking & Birdwatching: Explore nearby trails and hillsides, where toucans, tropical birds, and wildlife are part of your everyday scenery.
  • Water Sports: Easy access to Playa Veracruz, Amador Causeway, and the Pearl Islands for snorkeling, paddleboarding, sailing, whale watching, and fishing adventures.
  • Cycling Routes: Enjoy scenic cycling paths around Amador, Causeway, and nearby nature corridors, perfect for early morning rides.
  • Gastronomic Exploration: From fine dining inside Tucán and high-end restaurants in the city to casual beachfront spots in Veracruz, your culinary map is at your doorstep.

Casco Antiguo

The historic heart of Panama City, where colonial architecture, boutique hotels, vibrant plazas, and gourmet restaurants converge. By day, explore churches and museums; by night, enjoy rooftop bars with dazzling skyline views.

Canal de Panamá – Esclusas de Miraflores

Witness one of the world’s greatest engineering marvels in motion. From Palash Tower, you are just a short drive away from the Miraflores Locks visitor center and its panoramic views of massive ships crossing the Canal.

Cerro Ancón

A green hill overlooking the city and the Canal, ideal for morning hikes and dramatic viewpoints. From its summit, you can see the old quarter, the modern skyline, and the ships lining up for their Canal transit.

Amador Causeway

A scenic road stretching into the Pacific, connecting small islands with marinas, restaurants, bike paths, and oceanfront promenades. Perfect for sunsets, family walks, and weekend escapes.

Panamá Pacífico

A growing master-planned community across the Canal, with business parks, schools, and recreational areas. An emerging hub of innovation and quality of life.

Veracruz

A relaxed beachside area just minutes from the city, where you can enjoy fresh seafood by the sea, sunsets over the Pacific, and a laid-back coastal atmosphere.
